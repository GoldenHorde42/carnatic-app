Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

Carnatic ("we", "our", or "us") is a mobile application that helps users discover Carnatic classical music through curated YouTube videos. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

You can use the Carnatic app without creating an account. An account is optional and only needed for personalised recommendations and saved playlists.

Information We Collect

1. Account information (optional)

If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address from Google. We store this to identify your account and personalise your experience. We do not receive your Google password.

2. Watch history (signed-in users only)

We store which videos you have watched and approximate watch duration so we can surface relevant recommendations. This data is stored securely in our database and is never sold or shared with third parties.

3. Search queries

We store anonymised search queries (stripped of any personally identifiable information) to improve search quality. Queries are not linked to individual user accounts.

4. Usage analytics

We collect anonymised app usage data (screens visited, feature usage) to improve the product. This data is aggregated and not linked to your identity.

YouTube API Data

This app is Powered by YouTube. We use the YouTube Data API v3 to search for and display Carnatic music videos.

By using this app, you also agree to Google's Privacy Policy and the YouTube Terms of Service.

We cache basic video metadata (title, thumbnail URL, view count, duration) in our database to reduce API calls. We do not store video content itself — all playback happens through YouTube's embedded player.

We do not use YouTube API data to build user profiles for advertising purposes.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide personalised video recommendations based on your watch history
  • To remember your preferences within the app
  • To improve search relevance and app performance
  • To respond to your support requests

We do not:

  • Sell your data to advertisers or any third parties
  • Use your data for targeted advertising
  • Share your personal data without your consent, except as required by law

Data Retention

  • Watch history is retained for 12 months and then automatically deleted
  • If you delete your account, all personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Anonymised analytics data is retained for up to 24 months

Children's Privacy

The Carnatic app is designed for music students of all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. Users under 13 are encouraged to use the app without signing in — all core features (browse, search, playback) work without an account.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at the email below and we will delete it promptly.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of personalised recommendations by using the app without an account
  • Revoke Google sign-in access at any time via your Google account settings

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@carnaticapp.music. We will respond within 30 days.

Security

Your data is stored on Supabase (hosted on AWS), which provides encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.2+). We use Row-Level Security (RLS) policies to ensure users can only access their own data. We follow industry-standard security practices and review our security posture regularly.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google / YouTube — video search and playback
  • Supabase — database and authentication infrastructure
  • Groq — natural language search query processing (anonymised queries only)

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email (if you have an account). Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
